Last Z Official Store Guide

Economy support guide • Updated June 19, 2026 • Last Z Guides

Quick Answer

The Official Last Z Store / Gold Bar Store is worth checking every day, even if you are not a heavy spender. The biggest value is the monthly Surprise! package, buying Gold Bars through the website before spending them on packs, claiming daily Points tasks, and finishing the 7-Day Check-In so you do not miss the final weekly reward.

  1. 1 Surprise! package - one of the best price/value buys in the game.
  2. 2 Gold Bars first - buying Gold Bars on the website is usually better than buying packs directly in-game.
  3. 3 Extra tracks - website purchases advance Recharge Event and Membership Center rewards.
  4. 4 Daily claims - claim Points tasks and 7-Day Check-In rewards every day.

i Official store: open store.last-z.com and check the live rewards before spending.

What the Official Store Is For

The Official Last Z Store is worth opening for two jobs: claim free daily value, and make paid spending go further when you were already going to spend. Do not treat it like a random pack menu. The strongest routine is to collect the 7-Day Check-In, claim daily Points tasks, check Points Shop discounts, and only then look at paid offers.

If you spend, check the monthly Surprise! package before anything else. Current player experience puts it among the best price/value buys in the game, especially for light spenders who want one efficient purchase instead of scattered packs.

Gold Bars Before In-Game Packs

Buying Gold Bars through the Official Store and then spending those Gold Bars on packs is usually better value than buying the same kind of pack directly inside the game.

That does not mean every pack is good. It means the purchase route matters. If you already planned to spend, check whether the website Gold Bar route gives better total value before using the in-game purchase button.

Other Packs Are Mostly for Heavy Spenders

The website has other packs in the Item Purchase tab with value, but light spenders should be careful. In-game events and limited offers can sometimes beat normal store pack value, so do not treat every website pack as automatically efficient.

For F2P and light-spender accounts, the priority is still account safety and long-term growth: shield reserve, key events, strong discounts, and permanent progression. For heavy spenders, the store can be useful because the extra reward tracks stack on top of purchases they were already going to make.

Recharge Event and Membership Center

Website purchases advance the Recharge Event and Membership Center.

Recharge Event rewards matter because even early milestones add useful bonus rewards on top of the purchase. Membership Center matters because store spending builds longer-term membership progress, including weekly rewards and level rewards.

Before spending, check both screens:

  • Recharge Event: what milestone will this purchase reach?
  • Membership Center: what weekly or level reward does this move you toward?

Points Shop: Claim Daily Tasks

The Points Shop is one of the biggest reasons to open the Official Store every day. Daily tasks can give points, and those points can be saved for discounted items.

Prioritize high-value discounted items first. The best targets to watch for are:

  • Orange Hero Fragments
  • Wrenches
  • Badges
  • Orange Equipment

Do not spend points just because you have them. Claim the daily tasks, let points build up, then spend on the strongest discounted items when they appear.

7-Day Check-In

The 7-Day Check-In is free value. Open the Official Store every day and claim it.

The important part is consistency. The final weekly reward is the strongest one, so missing earlier days can cost more than it looks like. Make the store check part of your normal daily routine: claim Check-In, claim daily Points tasks, check Recharge/Membership if you are spending, then leave.

Best Daily Store Routine

  1. Open the Official Store.
  2. Claim the 7-Day Check-In.
  3. Complete and claim Points tasks.
  4. Check Points Shop discounts.
  5. If spending this month, check the Surprise! package first.
  6. If buying packs, compare Gold Bars through the website against direct in-game purchase value.
  7. Check Recharge Event and Membership Center before confirming a purchase.

What to Avoid

Avoid using the store as an excuse for random spending. The store is strongest when you already know what you are buying and why.

  • Do not buy normal packs before checking the monthly Surprise! package.
  • Do not ignore Points tasks.
  • Do not spend Points on weak items just because the balance is available.
  • Do not buy in-game packs before comparing the Gold Bar route.
  • Do not treat heavy-spender pack value as light-spender advice.

FAQ

Is the Official Last Z Store worth using?

Yes. Even non-heavy spenders should open it daily for the 7-Day Check-In and Points tasks. If you spend, the monthly Surprise! package and Gold Bar route are the first things to compare.

What is the best purchase in the Official Last Z Store?

The monthly Surprise! package is the strongest first check. Based on current player experience, it is one of the best price/value purchases in the game.

Should I buy packs through the website or inside the game?

If you already plan to spend, buying Gold Bars through the website and then using them for packs is usually better than buying packs directly in-game. Still compare the live offer before buying.

What should I use Points Shop points on?

Save points for high-value discounted items such as Orange Hero Fragments, Wrenches, Badges, and Orange Equipment.

Do I need to open the Official Store every day?

Yes. Claim the 7-Day Check-In and daily Points tasks. The final weekly check-in reward is the biggest reason to stay consistent.

Before You Spend

  • Open the live store and confirm the current Gold Bar, pack, Points Shop, Recharge Event, and Membership Center details before buying.
  • Use this page as spending priority guidance, not as a promise that every visible store pack is good value.
  • If you are F2P or a light spender, keep shield safety and long-term progression ahead of random pack value.
