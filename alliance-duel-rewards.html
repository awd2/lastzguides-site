Last Z Alliance Duel Rewards — Chest Thresholds, Daily Totals, and Weekly Totals

Updated January 28, 2026 • Reward tables • Last Z Guides • Checked with server 403 players

Checked in game with server 403 players: Alliance Duel rewards are easiest to plan by chest threshold. Compare Days 1-5, Day 6, daily totals, and weekly totals before spending saved items. Use these Alliance Duel reward tables to check chest thresholds for Days 1–5 and Day 6, compare daily and weekly totals, and see which reward checkpoints are worth planning around.

Alliance Duel Rewards at a Glance
  1. Days 1–5 use the standard 9-chest reward structure.
  2. Day 6 (Enemy Buster) uses different score thresholds and adds Valor Medals.
  3. Daily Total shows the full reward sum for all 9 chests on one day.
  4. Weekly Total shows what you get from five standard days plus Day 6.

For the full day-by-day schedule and scoring strategy, use the Alliance Duel Guide.

FAQ

What are the Last Z Alliance Duel chest thresholds?

Alliance Duel uses 9 reward chests per day. Days 1–5 follow the standard threshold table, while Day 6 (Enemy Buster) uses different score thresholds.

What is different on Day 6 (Enemy Buster)?

Day 6 uses its own Enemy Buster threshold table and adds Valor Medals to the reward pool, which makes it the most unique reward day of the week.

What do Daily Total and Weekly Total mean?

Daily Total is the sum of all 9 chests for one day. Weekly Total combines five standard Day 1–5 totals with the Day 6 total.

Which Alliance Duel reward chests matter most?

The later chests matter most because they contain the highest-value badge, alloy, skill book, and special reward payouts. Reaching the upper chests is where most of the real weekly value comes from.

How to Read the Alliance Duel Rewards Tables

  • Chest headers show the score threshold needed for each reward chest.
  • Reward rows show exactly which items increase as you push higher chests.
  • Daily Totals help you estimate one full day of value.
  • Weekly Totals help you judge whether a research or badge investment will pay back over time.

If you are using these totals to plan badge spending, see the Alliance Recognition Costs guide and the Lucky Discounter guide.

Before Chasing Reward Chests

  • Use these reward tables to plan the week, then check the live Alliance Duel reward screen before spending saved items.
  • Daily and weekly chest thresholds are useful for planning, but the in-game event screen is the final source for your server.
  • If rewards, shop stock, or scoring tasks changed on your server, follow the live event screen over this table.
