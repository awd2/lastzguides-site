Recommended Research Route

For most players, the cleanest badge route is to secure efficient early and mid-game value first. After Peace Shield/Urgent Rescue, do not treat Field Research as mandatory before UST/T10; choose the next branch by account goal.

  1. Hero Training to Cockpit
  2. Military Strategies
  3. Peace Shield / Urgent Rescue
  4. Shortest practical UST/T10 path for tier progression, or Siege to Seize for deep combat scaling
  5. Field Research only after 100% Siege to Seize for late Recharge Shield planning

Later pressure branches: Alliance Recognition, Army Building, Fully Armed Alliance, and Unit Special Training are not “do this immediately” pages. They are the branches where badge planning starts becoming a major account commitment.

How to Use This Atlas

Step 1: Compare total cost before you commit

Use the totals here to see whether a branch is a quick checkpoint or a multi-month badge sink. This prevents expensive branches from looking deceptively close just because the first few nodes are cheap.

Step 2: Open the branch tree page

Every card leads to a full branch page with the exact node tree, grouped costs, and the real checkpoint that matters before you finish the whole branch.

Step 3: Compare branch order with your account goal

If your account still needs efficient mid-game value, open Hero Training, Military Strategies, and Peace Shield first. If your goal is tier progression, compare the shortest practical UST/T10 path before committing to badge-heavy late branches. If your goal is deep combat scaling, move into Siege to Seize and Field Research only when the earlier route is secure.

Research Costs Atlas FAQ

Which research branch costs the most badges in Last Z?

Unit Special Training is the largest branch in the current cluster at 1,488,100 badges total.

Which research branch should I do first?

For most players, Hero Training is the first important branch and the efficient stop is Cockpit before shifting into Military Strategies.

What unlocks Field Research?

Field Research unlocks after you complete 100% Siege to Seize.

What comes after Peace Shield?

For the main account path, Peace Shield usually leads into Siege to Seize. Army Building is a separate later follow-up that requires Peace Shield to be fully complete.
