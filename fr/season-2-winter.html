Guide Season 2 Winter Last Z — Bio Lab, Greenhouse et Frost Cores

En Season 2 Winter, la priorité est le tempo de résistance saisonnière. Commencez par Smart Greenhouse et Bio Lab, farmez les Frost Cores via Frostbanes et autres cibles saisonnières, utilisez les Ice Blooms pour garder Bio Lab actif, et utilisez Mastery Hall pour les skills et buffs saisonniers. Coordonnez-vous avec l’alliance, car les villes capturées reset au changement de saison.

Ce qui change au démarrage de Season 2 Winter

Season 2 Winter changes the server from normal progression into a seasonal map race. Captured city control resets, the map adds winter targets, and your account starts building a seasonal resistance engine through Smart Greenhouse, Bio Lab, Ice Blooms, and Frost Cores.

Priorité des bâtiments en Season 2 Winter

The early building priority is not "upgrade everything." Your first job is to build the resource and resistance engine.

  1. Smart Greenhouse first: this is your Ice Bloom engine. If Greenhouses fall behind, Bio Lab upgrades start waiting on resource income.
  2. Bio Lab second, but never idle: Bio Lab is the main resistance gate. Push it whenever you have enough Ice Blooms and Frost Cores.
  3. Batiments ATK/DEF plus tard : Command Post, Icy Arsenal et Icy Fort sont plus forts une fois votre base de resistance en place. Chaque joueur les construit individuellement. Si vous les rushez trop tot, vous ralentissez les batiments qui debloquent un meilleur farming.
  4. Mastery Hall alongside the core: use Mastery Hall for seasonal skills and buffs. Treat it as part of your season progression plan, not as a random side building.

Simple early rule: if your next Bio Lab upgrade is blocked by Ice Blooms, improve Greenhouses. If Bio Lab can keep moving, keep Bio Lab moving.

Smart Greenhouse vs Bio Lab

Smart Greenhouse and Bio Lab work together. Greenhouses feed the system; Bio Lab turns that income into stronger seasonal progress.

Building Valeur principale Erreur à éviter
Smart Greenhouse Ice Bloom production for Bio Lab upgrades Leaving one Greenhouse far below the others and choking income
Bio Lab Major resistance progression and seasonal unlock tempo Spending Frost Cores elsewhere and letting Bio Lab sit idle

Pour la plupart des joueurs, l'objectif pratique est de garder Bio Lab en progression reguliere. Pour le timing exact de Bio Lab, Smart Greenhouse, Ice Blooms et des batiments Combat, utilisez le Guide Season 2 Bio Lab.

Créatures Season 2 Winter : Frostbanes, Snow Leopards et Yetis

Le farming de Season 2 Winter tourne autour des creatures saisonnieres et des tentatives limitees. Ne depensez pas fuel ou tentatives au hasard simplement parce qu'une nouvelle cible apparait sur la carte.

  • Frostbanes : c'est generalement votre premiere priorite, car ils alimentent les Frost Cores et la progression saisonniere. Faites ces rallies avant les cibles de moindre valeur quand les tentatives sont limitees.
  • Snow Leopards : utiles pour les first clears et le farming saisonnier, mais les niveaux plus hauts dependent de votre resistance. Si vous ne pouvez pas encore atteindre un niveau proprement, ameliorez d'abord le moteur Greenhouse / Bio Lab.
  • Yetis: limited-attempt targets with strong rewards. Coordinate rallies and do not ignore them when your alliance is organizing Yeti kills.

Si votre alliance donne un ordre de cibles, suivez-le. Season 2 recompense davantage la coordination que les tests solo. Pour les regles exactes de tentatives et de fuel, utilisez le Guide Season 2 Frost Core.

Frost Cores et Ice Blooms : ne gaspillez pas le moteur

Frost Cores and Ice Blooms are not just random seasonal resources. They decide whether your Bio Lab keeps moving or gets stuck.

  • Ice Blooms : produits par les Smart Greenhouses et depenses dans la progression des batiments saisonniers.
  • Frost Cores: earned from seasonal targets such as Frostbanes, Snow Leopards, and Yetis and used to keep key upgrades moving.

Before spending either resource on anything optional, check the next Bio Lab requirement. Falling behind on Bio Lab can lock you out of stronger creature levels and slow the whole season. The Season 2 Bio Lab guide covers the daily Bio Lab / Smart Greenhouse decision loop.

Pour les tentatives quotidiennes Frostbane, les couts en fuel, le timing Snow Leopard et la coordination Yeti, lisez le Guide Season 2 Frost Core.

Command Post, Icy Arsenal et Icy Fort

Season 2 Winter also adds player-built ATK/DEF buildings: Command Post, Icy Arsenal et Icy Fort. These matter for seasonal fighting, but they should not steal your entire early resource plan.

Early S2 is about unlocking resistance and farming better targets. Once your Greenhouse and Bio Lab base is healthy, Command Post, Icy Arsenal et Icy Fort become more attractive for rallies, city fights, Glory War, and other seasonal PvP pressure.

Si vous etes F2P ou petit depensier, soyez particulierement prudent ici. Rusher les batiments ATK/DEF peut sembler fort sur le moment, tout en retardant discretement le niveau de Bio Lab qui debloquerait un meilleur farming et une meilleure resistance.

For the exact Bio Lab 28 breakpoint, Smart Greenhouse 25-26 handoff, and per-building values, use the Season 2 Combat Buildings guide.

Mastery Hall et skills saisonniers

Mastery Hall est le batiment de Season 2 pour les skills et buffs saisonniers. Ne l'ignorez pas, car les skills saisonniers peuvent changer votre vitesse de progression dans les systemes Winter et l'utilite de votre compte pour l'alliance.

The practical rule is to check Mastery Hall before spending season resources elsewhere. If a skill or buff improves your seasonal economy, resistance path, or alliance combat value, it can be worth more than another low-impact building level.

Utilisez Mastery Hall comme checkpoint de planification : Greenhouse et Bio Lab construisent votre moteur de resistance, tandis que Mastery Hall transforme la progression de saison en bonus saisonniers plus durables. Pour le timing Support vs Combat, les decisions de reset et les skills nommes, utilisez le Guide Mastery Hall.

Season 2 Winter et city reset

Captured city control resets when Season 2 starts. That makes the first phase of S2 an alliance coordination problem, not just a personal upgrade problem.

Before attacking or moving, check your alliance plan: target cities, rally leaders, NAP rules, shield expectations, and where weaker members should stand. Random solo movement can waste troops or break agreements.

If your alliance is organized, your job is to follow the route and keep your account ready: enough shields, enough resources, and enough event discipline to keep growing while the map is being contested.

Événements et systèmes Season 2 Winter

Season 2 Winter peut inclure des systemes et events comme Snowman Mania, Frozen Rescue, Arctic Fishing et Glory War. Ne les traitez pas comme des choses separees de votre plan de batiments : beaucoup de taches saisonnieres vous ramènent aux kills de creatures, aux Ice Blooms, aux Frost Cores ou au timing d'alliance.

La regle cle est de verifier la valeur de l'event avant de depenser. Si un event demande des ressources de heros, utilisez le Guide Hero Recruitment et le Planning des evenements pour synchroniser vos depenses avec de meilleures fenetres de recompenses.

Que faire avant le début de Season 2

  • Create any farm account you still need. Same-server farm creation may become restricted after Season 2 starts.
  • Clear builder queues close to season launch. You want builders available for Smart Greenhouse and Bio Lab instead of locked into long low-impact upgrades.
  • Save flexible speed-ups. Seasonal buildings build quickly at first, but speed-ups still matter when you are trying to keep Bio Lab moving.
  • Save event-sensitive hero resources. Prime Recruitment Tickets, hero fragments, Power Cores, Enhancement Alloys, and skill books should still be timed around event windows.
  • Ask your alliance for the city plan. City control resets, so do not assume old ownership or old rally routes apply.
  • Keep shields ready. Season starts create more map movement and more mistakes.

Season 2 Winter vs anciens guides Desert

For current planning, use Season 2 Winter. Some older guides and community comments still mention Season 2 Desert, but that should not drive your current account plan unless your own game screen clearly shows Desert.

The useful part of old season advice is the general warning that seasons change the map and alliance priorities. The risky part is following old Desert-specific buildings, resources, or tile advice as if it still applies to Winter.

FAQ Season 2 Winter

Que faut-il améliorer en premier en Season 2 Winter ?

Start with Smart Greenhouse and Bio Lab. Greenhouse supports Ice Bloom income, while Bio Lab drives the resistance tempo that unlocks better seasonal farming.

Quelles sont les ressources clés de Season 2 Winter ?

The main early resources to watch are Ice Blooms and Frost Cores. Before spending them on optional upgrades, check the next Bio Lab requirement.

Quelles créatures Season 2 Winter comptent d’abord ?

Priorisez Frostbanes en premier quand les tentatives ou le fuel sont limites, puis utilisez Snow Leopards et Yetis selon la resistance, les tentatives quotidiennes et les consignes de l'alliance.

Les villes capturées reset-elles au début de Season 2 ?

Yes. Captured city control resets at season change, so alliances need a fresh city plan for Season 2 instead of relying on the previous map state.

Faut-il suivre les anciens guides Season 2 Desert ?

Utilisez les anciens guides Desert seulement comme contexte. Pour la planification actuelle, suivez Season 2 Winter sauf si votre écran Season en jeu indique clairement autre chose.
