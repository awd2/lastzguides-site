Last Z Refugees Guide - Priority, Tickets, and Best Refugees to Upgrade

Progression Guide • Updated February 1, 2026 • 7 min read • Last Z Guides • Checked with server 403 players

Quick Answer

Best Last Z refugee priority and upgrade strategy: prioritize Diplomats first, use 500-ticket summons with free refreshes as your default ticket plan, and only push 2000/5000 tickets when a top-tier refugee is worth chasing.

  1. ① Recruit at the Club · Refugee Tickets
  2. ② Assign via Dispatch · or Talent Hub
  3. ③ Diplomats → Alliance Center · help cap + duration
  4. ④ 500-ticket + refresh loop · community-favored approach
  5. ⑤ Spend on Shelter Upgrade day · if Alliance Duel is active

ℹ️ Core rule: refugees are strongest when they improve daily account efficiency, so long-term utility usually beats short-term niche picks.

🤝 Top priority: Diplomats are usually the best refugees because alliance help value compounds every day.

🎟️ Best default: 500-ticket summons plus free refreshes give the safest long-term value for most players.

What Are Refugees in Last Z?

Refugees are long-term account specialists that improve specific buildings and systems. If you are deciding who to upgrade first, the key question is not rarity alone — it is which refugee gives repeatable value to your account every day.

You recruit them at the Club with Refugee Tickets, then assign them via the Dispatch button or the Talent Hub. Promotions use Refugee Fragments to improve their bonuses.

For broader diamond and long-term spending priority, see the F2P Guide.

Best Refugees to Prioritize First

Most refugee value comes from daily account efficiency, not niche bonuses. That is why community priority usually starts with Diplomats and other utility refugees before narrower role-specific picks.

  1. Tier 1: Diplomats
  2. Tier 2: Officer, Nurse, Scientist, Butler
  3. Tier 3: Rider / Assaulter / Shooter Instructors
  4. Tier 4: Metallurgist

This is a player-verified priority list based on long-term value (help cap, research, construction, and training speed).

Refugee Ticket Strategy: 500 vs 2000 vs 5000

The biggest refugee mistake is overspending tickets too early. For most players, the 500-ticket plus free refresh approach creates better long-term control unless a top-tier refugee makes deeper spending clearly worth it.

  • Use 500 tickets to summon, then use the free refresh to roll new options
  • 2000/5000 ticket pushes are usually reserved for top-tier refugees
  • Some guides recommend spending diamonds for priority refugees; many community strategies avoid diamond spending unless you are chasing a top-tier roll

Costs scale from 500 to 2000 and 5000 tickets if you keep pushing the same pool. Choose your budget and stick to it.

Why Diplomats Are the Best Refugees

Diplomats are usually the top refugee priority because alliance help value compounds into construction, research, healing, and other daily systems. That makes them one of the strongest long-term account upgrades in the whole refugee pool.

Diplomats are assigned to the Alliance Center. They increase alliance help cap, and in-game lists also show help duration bonuses. This makes construction, research, and healing much faster through ally help.

💡 Player example: 16 hours of help time can heal ~1,300 troops in a minute using alliance help.

Refugee Roles and Building Assignments

Understanding refugee roles matters because the best pick depends on where your account is bottlenecked. Some refugees improve universal daily systems, while others are much narrower and should come later.

  • Diplomat: Alliance Center (alliance help cap)
  • Officer: Military Center (unit cap)
  • Scientist: Bookstore (research speed + free research speedup time)
  • Butler: Villa (construction speed)
  • Nurse: Hospital (healing speed)
  • Assault/Rider/Shooter Instructor: camps (training speed)
  • Metallurgist: Smelting Plant (Enhancement Alloy production)
  • Community Manager: Residence (Zents production)
  • Farmer/Lumberjack/Mechanic: Farmhouse/Lumberyard/Wind Turbine (resource production)
  • Stamina Trainer: Training Ground (Heroic Experience production)
  • Warehouse Keeper: Warehouse (resource protection)

Best Sources of Refugee Tickets

Ticket income is limited, which is why spending discipline matters. The best refugee strategy depends as much on ticket sourcing as it does on target priority.

  • Fury Lord rewards
  • Glory Shop (weekly 5,000 tickets when available)
  • Packs as optional top-ups

For one of the main repeatable ticket sources, use the Furylord Guide.

When to Spend Refugee Tickets

Timing matters because refugee summons can overlap with Alliance Duel scoring. In most cases you want both: strong target selection and event timing when it does not force a bad roll decision.

The Alliance Duel Shelter Upgrade day includes recruiting refugees for points. If you can time your summons to that day, it boosts your score. However, if a top-tier refugee appears, many guides recommend taking it immediately.

For the daily routine that gets the most value out of these bonuses, use the Daily Checklist.

FAQ

Which refugees should I prioritize?

Most players should prioritize Diplomats first, then high-value utility refugees like Officer, Nurse, Scientist, and Butler. Instructors and narrower roles usually come later.

Should I spend 2000/5000 tickets on refugees?

Only if you are chasing a top-tier refugee (high-rarity Diplomat, Butler, Scientist). Most community strategies prefer 500-ticket summons + free refresh unless the roll is elite.

What do Diplomats do?

Diplomats are assigned to the Alliance Center and increase alliance help cap. In-game lists also show help duration bonuses, which speeds construction, research, and healing through alliance help.

How do I get refugee tickets?

Main repeatable sources include Furylord rewards and Glory Shop tickets when available, with packs as optional extra sources.

Why are Diplomats the best refugees in Last Z?

Because alliance help value compounds across construction, research, healing, and other daily systems. That makes Diplomats one of the strongest long-term efficiency upgrades in the refugee pool.

What is the best default refugee ticket strategy?

For most players, the best default is 500-ticket summons plus free refreshes, unless a top-tier refugee makes a deeper 2000 or 5000-ticket push clearly worthwhile.

Before Spending Refugee Tickets

  • Use the 500-ticket plus refresh loop unless a top-tier roll clearly justifies a deeper push.
  • Before spending diamonds or saved items, check the live refugee roll, current event value, and your resource reserve.
  • If your server pressure is high, protect shields and core resources before chasing a marginal refugee upgrade.
