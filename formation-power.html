Last Z Formation Power Guide — How to Reach 100M+ Power

Formation Guide • Updated May 23, 2026 • 18 min read • Last Z Guides • Checked with server 403 players

Quick Answer

Best way to raise formation power: keep same-faction bonuses intact for the displayed power number, then stack troop HP research, Hall of Honor, march capacity, vehicle upgrades, and live map bonuses before chasing smaller hero swaps.

  1. ① 5 same-faction heroes · removing 1 drops 72M → 31M
  2. ② Troop HP research · Recharge Shield = +7.5M power
  3. ③ Hall of Honor · prioritize troop-capacity heroes by faction
  4. ④ Siege buildings · +6M power on world map
  5. ⑤ March capacity · Licia +7.5%, Oliveira +10%

💡 #1 rule: Never break faction bonus — it's worth more than individual hero stats.

ℹ️ Note: 5 same-faction maximizes power stat, but 3+2 provides better counter advantage in PvP. Choose based on your priority. See Formations Guide for 3+2 builds.

Understanding Formation Power

Power Types Are Different

Your power changes by context (arena vs map):

  • HQ Power: What you see in your base (includes decorations)
  • Arena Power: ~66M (no siege building bonuses)
  • Map/Rally Power: ~72M (includes siege building bonuses)
  • Formation Power: Actual combat power with all buffs

Example: Same account shows 66M in arena but 72M on map — that's 6M from siege buildings alone!

#1 Rule: Use 5 Heroes of Same Faction

The Biggest Power Multiplier

This is the single most important tip for formation power.

Real example:

  • 5 Blood Rose heroes: 72 million power
  • Remove 1 hero: 31 million power
  • Difference: 41 million power lost!

Why Same Faction Matters

When you deploy 5 heroes of the same faction, you activate:

  • Katrina 4th skill: +10% troop HP (all troops)
  • Faction bonus: +15% attack
  • Other hero 4th skills: +10% attack/defense
  • Licia bonus: +7.5% damage resistance + troop capacity

Total bonuses: These stack to give massive multipliers that don't apply with mixed factions. See our Formations Guide for optimal setups.

Even Blue Heroes Beat Orange from Other Factions

Surprising fact: A blue hero from your faction can give MORE formation power than an orange hero from a different faction.

Why? The faction bonuses from 5 same-faction heroes outweigh individual hero stats.

Example: Audrey (blue, Blood Rose) vs Scarlett (orange, Wings of Dawn) — Audrey gives higher formation power when completing the 5-Blood Rose bonus.

Troop HP Research — The Power King

Recharge Shield Research

This research gives the highest formation power per badge spent.

  • Located in: Field Research tree (final node)
  • 1% troop HP = ~150-160k formation power
  • Maximum: 10% HP increase (costs 102,300 badges)
  • Total potential: 7.5+ million combat power

HP Research Priority

Research HP in this order based on your faction:

  • Blood Rose: Assaulter HP first
  • Wings of Dawn: Shooter HP first
  • Guard of Order: Rider HP first
  • Then: General Troop HP (applies to all)

Note: If switching factions (e.g., Blood Rose to Wings of Dawn), your power will drop until you complete the new faction's HP research.

Fully Armed Alliance Research

The ultimate goal for combat power:

  • Final research: +25 troop size (max 500 total)
  • Troop HP bonus: +10% (independent of troop type)
  • Total combat power boost: ~7.5 million
  • Troop size bonus: Only active during Enemy Buster day

Badge cost: ~1-1.5 million badges to complete. This is why Alliance Recognition must be maxed first.

Hall of Honor — Hidden Power Source

What is Hall of Honor?

A building system that gives permanent buffs when you upgrade maxed heroes.

Unlock requirements:

  • Toxic Fog Garden level 20: Blood Rose Hall of Honor
  • Dawn Tower level 20: Wings of Dawn Hall of Honor
  • Steel Factory level 20: Guard of Order Hall of Honor

Hero requirement: Hero must be maxed (level 150, 5-star) to appear in Hall of Honor.

Hall of Honor Bonuses

Each hero upgraded in Hall of Honor gives:

  • Faction hero attack % — e.g., Blood Rose hero attack +10% at level 200
  • All hero attack — Affects ALL heroes regardless of faction
  • Hero troop capacity — Capacity milestones on heroes such as Vivian, Christina, and Maria are the strongest Hall of Honor formation-power targets

Stat Multipliers by Hero Rarity

Orange heroes:ATK or DEF +5 per level
Purple heroes:ATK or DEF +2 per level
Blue heroes:ATK or DEF +1 per level

Multiplier: ~2.5x difference between tiers

Hall of Honor Upgrade Priority

The strongest Hall of Honor formation-power route is to focus hero troop capacity heroes inside the faction you actually use. For Blood Rose, that means Vivian first; use the same logic for Christina and Maria in their factions.

Upgrade material: Hall of Honor levels use Hero Fragments and Universal Hero Fragments. Fragments are required on every level, so do not spread them randomly across low-impact heroes.

Faction Building Bonuses

Upgrading faction buildings (Toxic Fog Garden, etc.) also gives:

  • Hero attack — Faction-specific
  • Hero defense — Faction-specific
  • Troop capacity: +1 per level (up to 26), +5 per level (27+)

Modified Vehicle Power

Vehicle Upgrade Strategy

  • Level 100: Unlock components (massive stat boosts)
  • Component levels: Level 15 = 200% troop defense in siege
  • Modifications: Use golden wrenches for damage, HP, defense stats

Expert Modifier Warning

Before upgrading to Expert Modifier, save 200k modification blueprints!

  • When you activate Expert Modifier, you get a 30-minute window
  • During this window: 100 attempts give double EXP
  • Need 160k+ blueprints to maximize this opportunity

For activation timing and blueprint prep details, see the Vehicle Modification Cost Table.

Don't accidentally activate it without enough blueprints!

Monday Upgrade Day

Upgrade all modified vehicle components on Monday (Modded Vehicle Boost day):

  • Get Alliance Duel points
  • Get Full Preparedness points
  • Maximize value from your wrenches and blueprints

Siege Buildings — Hidden 6M Power

Three Siege Buildings

These buildings add power ONLY on the map (not in arena):

  • Strategy Clubrack — Troop attack ability
  • Military Fortress — Troop defense ability
  • Defensive Base — Damage bonuses

Combined bonus: ~6 million formation power on map battles!

Why Arena Power is Lower

Arena power doesn't include:

  • Siege building bonuses
  • Some modified vehicle skill effects
  • Map-specific buffs

Don't panic if arena power looks lower than map power — this is normal.

Research Priority for Formation Power

Step-by-Step Research Order

  1. Badge-free research: New Home, Shelter Building, Elite Troops, HQ Management, Rapid Growth
  2. Alliance Recognition — FIRST. Doubles all badge rewards. Required for everything else.
  3. Hero Training — Least badge cost among badge-requiring research
  4. Troop HP research — Recharge Shield, faction-specific HP
  5. Fully Armed Alliance — End-game goal (1-1.5M badges)

Badge Management

With maxed Alliance Recognition:

  • ~3k badges daily increase (passive)
  • Can maintain 100k+ badges while doing research
  • No need to buy badges from Lucky Discounter

Without maxed Alliance Recognition:

  • Constant badge shortage
  • Forced to spend diamonds on badges
  • Can't progress efficiently

When to Use Badges

  • Day 3 (Age of Science): Badge research for Alliance Duel points
  • Normal days: Only badge-free research (Rapid Growth, Elite Troops)

Equipment & Hero Optimization

Equipment Priority Stats

Focus on these stats in equipment:

  • Damage — Increases troop damage dealt
  • HP — Increases troop survivability
  • Attack/Defense — General stat boosts

Troop Type Alignment

Match your troops to your faction for maximum buffs:

  • Blood Rose: Assaulter troops (Selena boosts assaulter attack)
  • Wings of Dawn: Shooter troops
  • Guard of Order: Rider troops

T9/T10 priority: Upgrade your faction's troop type first.

T10 Timeline

For non-spenders/small spenders:

  • ~1.5 years from game start to unlock T10
  • Requires Lab level 30
  • Requires ~1-1.5 million badges

Focus on: T9 first, then promote all troops to highest tier available.

Frequently Asked Questions

What gives the most formation power?

Troop HP research (Recharge Shield) gives ~150k power per 1%. Using 5 same-faction heroes activates massive bonuses worth 40M+ power. Hall of Honor upgrades and siege buildings add millions more.

How do I increase arena power (and why is it different from map power)?

Arena doesn't include siege building bonuses. Map power includes Strategy Clubrack, Military Fortress, and Defensive Base buffs — 6+ million power difference.

What is Hall of Honor?

Hall of Honor unlocks from faction buildings at level 20. Maxed level 150, 5-star heroes can appear there. The strongest formation-power route is to prioritize faction heroes with hero troop capacity milestones, such as Vivian for Blood Rose, because those capacity bonuses apply to that faction's heroes.

How do I increase march capacity?

Hero levels (+350), hero stars (+150), hero skills (Licia +7.5%, Oliveira +10%), technologies (+20-500), and faction buildings (+5 troops per level after 27). Can reach 3,500+ troops per hero.

Should I use 5 heroes of the same faction?

Absolutely YES. Removing 1 hero can drop power from 72M to 31M. Faction bonuses (+10% HP, +15% attack, etc.) are the biggest power multipliers in the game.

When does Fully Armed Alliance troop bonus apply?

The +500 troop size bonus only applies during Enemy Buster day. On normal days, you won't see this bonus in your march capacity.
