How to Increase Power Fast in Last Z

Power Guide • Updated February 1, 2026 • 15 min read • Last Z Guides • Checked with server 403 players

Based on videos: How to Increase Your Power 4x Faster, and How to Increase Your Power 2x Faster.

Quick Answer

Best way to increase power fast in Last Z: fix the bottleneck that blocks your next upgrade. Start with HQ and Lab if unlocks are stuck; then push core research, constant troop training, main 5 heroes, and vehicle level 100.

  1. ① Structure · HQ + Lab together, always
  2. ② Tech · core path + Alliance Recognition checkpoints
  3. ③ Troops · train constantly, highest tier
  4. ④ Heroes · orange equipment + skills priority
  5. ⑤ Modified Vehicle · level 100 for components

💡 Key rule: do not chase empty CP. Upgrade the system that is currently blocking unlocks, combat value, troop count, hero output, or vehicle components.

🏗️ Best early power: HQ + Lab.

📚 Best research mindset: balance core combat branches with Alliance Recognition checkpoints.

🚚 Best long-term power: troops + heroes + vehicle.

What Increases Combat Power in Last Z?

Combat Power is split across five buckets. The fastest upgrade is the one that removes the current blocker, not the one that only raises the visible number.

Your total Combat Power (CP) comes from 5 sources:

  • Structure Power — Buildings (HQ, Lab, camps)
  • Tech Power — Research completion
  • Troop Power — Army size and tier
  • Hero Power — Hero levels, stars, skills, equipment
  • Modified Vehicle Power — Vehicle level, mods, components
If this is blocked... Push this next
New buildings, troop tiers, or research are locked HQ and Laboratory
You have badges but weak combat stats Hero Training to Cockpit, Military Strategies, and Peace Shield
Training queues sit idle or troop tier is behind Troop training and Elite Troops path
You lose fights despite decent CP Main 5 heroes, orange gear, and combat skills
Vehicle components are still locked Vehicle level 100 path

Real example: With 48M army power but 10.3M structure power, you can beat 98-99% of players. Don't neglect any power type.

Fastest Power Gains #1: Structure Power

Start with HQ and Laboratory because they unlock the rest of the account. If either one is behind, new research, troop tiers, buildings, and event scoring windows all slow down.

Headquarters — The Core Building

HQ is the heartbeat of your base. Upgrading it unlocks:

  • New buildings
  • Higher level troops
  • New researches
  • Big CP boost

Rule: Always upgrade HQ ASAP, then immediately start the required Laboratory. See our HQ Upgrade Guide for requirements.

Laboratory — Compulsory Building

Critical: You cannot upgrade HQ without upgrading Laboratory first. It's a hard requirement at every level.

  • As you finish HQ level 23, instantly start Lab level 23
  • Don't waste speed-ups if you don't have resources for the next Lab
  • Second Laboratory (purchased) — don't prioritize upgrading it

Other HQ Requirements

Each HQ level requires a second building besides Lab:

  • Can be any of the 3 camps (Assaulter, Shooter, Rider)
  • Can be Alliance Center
  • Can be Event Formation
  • Example: HQ 24 needs Assaulter Camp 23, HQ 25 needs Shooter Camp

Tip: Ask higher-level players what's needed for future levels to prepare in advance.

Resource Buildings — DON'T Over-Upgrade

Critical warning: Don't upgrade resource buildings beyond level 20!

  • Output at high levels: ~800 resources/hour
  • Weekly production: ~1.3-1.5 million resources
  • Cost to upgrade: 16+ million resources
  • ROI: 4+ months to recover costs

Resource buildings give CP, but the resource cost is not worth it. Focus elsewhere.

Building Event Sync

Always sync building upgrades with events:

  • Shelter Upgrade event — Points for building + speed-ups
  • Full Preparedness event — Additional rewards at Apocalypse Time 08:00 and 20:00
  • Don't finish buildings randomly — wait for building event day

Fastest Power Gains #2: Research Power

Research should answer one question: does the next badge spend unlock more combat value or more event value? Build Alliance Recognition checkpoints when they improve your weekly rewards, but compare each deeper push against Hero Training, Military Strategies, and Peace Shield.

Research Priority Order

  1. New Home research — Cheap, gives 10% construction + 10% research speed. Single research path, no branching. Do this FIRST.
  2. Shelter Building research — More stats, helps you research and build faster
  3. Elite Troops research — Doesn't consume badges! Do this before badge-heavy research
  4. Alliance Recognition checkpoints — build event value, then compare the next badge spend against Hero Training, Military Strategies, and Peace Shield

For the full order and badge logic, see the Research Priority Guide and the Alliance Recognition Costs page.

Alliance Recognition — Important, Not Automatic First

Do not treat Alliance Recognition as a reason to ignore every combat branch. It is valuable because it improves event rewards, but the right stopping point depends on whether your account still needs core combat value.

Benefits:

  • Double rewards — All daily chest rewards doubled
  • Unlocks chests 4-9 — Access to all Alliance Duel rewards
  • Event King research — +200% points earned
  • Event Expert research — Additional +100% points
  • Total: 300% extra points when maxed

Cost: use the exact Alliance Recognition Costs page before deciding whether the next checkpoint is worth more than combat research.

Fully Armed & Alliance Recognition Trees

These two research trees are specifically for Alliance Duel event. They won't be available during your first week/month.

Do not blindly ignore combat research. If Hero Training to Cockpit, Military Strategies, or Peace Shield are still missing, compare those branches before pushing Alliance Recognition deeper.

Once that foundation is done, use the exact branch pages for targeted planning: Hero Training Costs, Military Strategies Costs, Peace Shield Costs, and UST Costs.

Fastest Power Gains #3: Troop Power

Troop power comes from two habits: keep queues running and unlock better troop tiers. Random speedup bursts help less than never letting camps sit idle.

Train Constantly

Simple rule: Higher quantity = higher power. Train troops non-stop.

  • Queue training 24/7
  • Use training speed-ups on Friday (Alliance Duel Training day)
  • Focus on getting T10 troops (requires Lab level 30)

Military Center

Upgrade Military Center to increase unit capacity:

  • Higher level = more troops you can train
  • Example: Level 23 = 13,300 capacity, Level 24 = 14,300
  • Priority: Focus on T10 troops before pushing Military Center too high

Training Speed Boosts

Multiple sources increase training speed:

  • Research: Assembly Tech, Assault Training Speed (+10%)
  • Refugees: Assaulter camp refugees, training speed refugees
  • Heroes: Season 1 heroes boost specific troop training
  • Blue heroes: Support heroes with training buffs

Fastest Power Gains #4: Hero Power

Hero power is easy to waste because every hero can consume resources. Spend on the main 5 first: levels, stars, skill books, orange gear, and combat-focused skills.

Hero Power Sources

  • Level (use EXP)
  • Star rank (use fragments)
  • Skill levels (use skill books)
  • Equipment (use power cores, alloys)

Focus on Main 5 Heroes

Only upgrade your main formation heroes. Don't spread resources thin.

Skill priority: Not all skills are worth maxing!

  • S+ tier heroes (Yu Chan, Licia, Selena, etc.) — Max everything (core combat heroes)
  • Sophia (A tier) — Don't max 3rd skill (construction only) unless building focus
  • Katrina (B+ tier) — Mainly for her global passive; not a priority DPS hero
  • Blue heroes — Only upgrade for global passives (training/gathering speed)

Equipment Strategy

Focus ONLY on orange equipment. See our complete Gear Guide for upgrade priority.

  • You'll get 20+ orange equipment through various methods
  • Purple/blue equipment can be consumed later to upgrade orange
  • Keep all heroes at similar equipment levels to avoid conflicts
  • Power cores needed for promotion — save them

Where to get orange equipment:

  • Merit Shop — 1 orange equipment per week
  • Arena Shop — Orange equipment choice boxes
  • Exploration — Orange equipment at higher stages

Blue & Purple Heroes

Only upgrade on Thursday (Hero Initiative day in Alliance Duel)

  • Blue heroes have useful buffs (gathering speed, trading speed)
  • Wait to upgrade stars before skills (star gates skill levels)
  • Can't use blue heroes in battle — they're support only

Fastest Power Gains #5: Vehicle Power

Vehicle power becomes a serious spike once components unlock. The practical checkpoint is level 100; before that, keep progress moving without starving HQ, research, troops, or main heroes.

Vehicle Level 100 — The Goal

Get your vehicle to level 100 to unlock components.

  • Components give massive stat boosts
  • Level 7 components achievable without spending
  • Component stat scaling:
    • Level 12: 114% troop defense in siege
    • Level 13: 140%
    • Level 14: 166%
    • Level 15: 200%

Modifications (Golden Wrenches)

Use golden wrenches to increase stats. Farm them from Gacha Go events:

  • Troop damage resistance
  • All hero defense
  • Infield troop defense (tank vs tank)
  • Troop HP
  • All hero attack

Expert Modifier Warning

Don't upgrade to Expert Modifier yet!

  • There's an unlock option coming for 100 attempts in 30 minutes (double EXP)
  • Requires 160k modification blueprints
  • Save your blueprints for this unlock event

See the Vehicle Modification Cost Table for activation timing and blueprint prep. For mythic gear planning, use the Mythic Equipment overview.

For exact wrench totals, milestone unlocks, and modifier breakpoints, use the Vehicle Modification Costs guide.

Indirect Methods

Resource Management

  • Gather constantly — world map gathering is far more efficient than base production, especially with multiple marches and gathering bonuses.
  • Radar tasks — Gives versatile fragments, alloys, power cores
  • Alliance Duel gathering — Get points just for gathering

Refugees

Refugees boost everything:

  • Book Store refugees — Research speed (+8-25%)
  • Construction refugees — Building speed
  • Assaulter Camp refugees — Training speed (most important long-term)
  • Military Center refugees — Unit cap increase

Keep recruiting and upgrading refugee stars!

Exploration

Higher stages = better rewards:

  • Orange equipment choice boxes
  • Power cores (at higher levels)
  • Purple equipment boxes
  • Hero EXP
  • Resources

Arena Participation

Participate daily regardless of rank:

  • Top 400 hero power — Peak Arena (better rewards)
  • Below top 400 — Silver Arena
  • Arena Shop: Buy orange equipment choice boxes, power cores
  • If extra medals: versatile orange fragments

For the daily routine, easy-target logic, and Arena Shop mindset, use the Arena Guide.

VIP Levels

Goal: VIP 10 minimum

  • VIP 8: Purple hero fragments, purple skill books daily
  • VIP 9: +5% march speed, +10% fuel restoration
  • VIP 10: Versatile orange fragment, power cores, orange skill book daily
  • VIP 12: Troop capacity, attack/defense bonuses
  • VIP 14+: Nearly impossible for small spenders

Strong Alliance Benefits

Being in an active alliance gives:

  • Boomer rewards — Speed-ups, healing speed-ups, training speed-ups, resources
  • Pack purchase rewards — When alliance members buy packs, everyone gets rewards
  • Alliance research — Higher research = better buffs
  • City captures — 1,500+ diamonds per city capture
  • Alliance Duel rewards — 6 versatile fragments + 1,500 badges for weekly victory
  • Alliance teleports — Active alliances provide them easily

  • Resource buildings max level 20 — ROI is terrible beyond that
  • Research balance — Core combat branches first when missing; Alliance Recognition checkpoints when they improve event economy
  • Train troops non-stop — Queue 24/7

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the fastest way to increase power in Last Z?

Fix the current bottleneck. If unlocks are blocked, push HQ and Lab. If combat value is low, push core research. If queues are idle, train troops. If fights are weak, invest in the main 5 heroes. If components are locked, move the vehicle toward level 100.

What research gives the biggest power increase?

For most players: New Home first, then Shelter Building and Elite Troops, then balance Alliance Recognition checkpoints with combat branches such as Hero Training, Military Strategies, and Peace Shield.

Should I upgrade resource buildings?

Usually not beyond level 20. They give visible CP, but the return on resources is poor compared with HQ, Lab, camps, research, troops, and hero progression.

What VIP level should I aim for?

VIP 10 minimum for versatile orange fragments and power cores daily. VIP 12 gives troop bonuses. VIP 14+ is nearly impossible for small spenders.

How important is vehicle power in Last Z?

Very important. Vehicle progression becomes one of the biggest mid- and late-game power spikes, especially once you reach level 100 and unlock components.

Which heroes should I upgrade?

Only your main 5 formation heroes. Not all skills are worth maxing — skip construction/exploration skills if you don't need them. Focus on combat-relevant skills.

What are the 5 power types in Last Z?

The five power types are structure, research, troop, hero, and vehicle power. Use them as a checklist: the lowest or most blocked category is usually the next useful upgrade.

Which power source should I focus on first?

Start with HQ and Lab if they block unlocks. After that, choose the slowest useful system: research for stats, troop queues for capacity, main heroes for battle output, or vehicle level 100 for components.
