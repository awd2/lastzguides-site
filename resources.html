Last Z Resources Guide - Farm Food, Steel, and Diamonds

Resource Guide • Updated February 1, 2026 • 7 min read • Last Z Guides • Checked with server 403 players

Quick Answer

Best way to get resources fast in Last Z: run full gathering marches during downtime, clear daily tasks and bounties, keep production buildings at requirement levels, save chests for bulk openings, and treat steel and farm accounts as later bottleneck tools.

  1. ① Gather overnight · send all marches before sleeping
  2. ② Skip resource buildings · gathering is 10-50x better
  3. ③ Save chests · open 10k+ in bulk for 20k+ diamonds
  4. ④ Steel from bounties/nodes · plus mine, shops, events
  5. ⑤ Daily essentials · trucks, bounty, tasks

💡 Key rule: world gathering is usually much stronger than passive resource buildings, so builder time should stay focused on progression requirements.

🪙 Steel source: orange bounties, Steel Mine, steel nodes, Hub Shop, Furylord, and seasonal events matter once HQ upgrades get expensive.

📦 Diamond trick: bulk chest openings are one of the main free-to-play ways to spike diamond income.

What Resources Matter Most in Last Z?

Food, wood, and electricity are daily fuel. Steel is a separate late-game bottleneck. Diamonds are reserve currency, so spend them only when the event or shop value justifies it.

Best Gathering Strategy for Fast Resources

Optimize gathering before production. A full overnight march on good nodes usually beats hours of passive building output, especially when gathering heroes and alliance territory bonuses are active.

Overnight Gathering

The most efficient way to get resources:

  1. Before sleeping, send ALL marches to gather
  2. Choose high-level nodes (more resources)
  3. Use heroes with gathering speed bonuses
  4. Fill marches completely — don't send half-empty
  5. Collect in the morning

Gathering Heroes

These heroes have gathering speed bonuses (keep at 4-star for global effect):

  • Giselle — Wood gathering speed
  • Athena — General gathering bonus
  • Blue heroes — Various gathering passives

Tip: You don't need to deploy gathering heroes — their 4th skill global effect works automatically.

Node Selection

  • Higher level nodes = more resources per hour
  • Closer nodes = less travel time
  • Alliance territory = gathering speed bonus
  • Avoid contested areas = risk of attack

How to Get More Diamonds from Chests

Use chest saving as a bonus diamond strategy, not a daily income plan. Bulk opening does not change the odds per chest, but it gives more chances at rare drops and makes jackpot results easier to notice.

Chest Type Possible Rewards Notes
Bounty Mission Chests Resources (90%), 100-5,000 diamonds, orange fragments Most common chest, 5k diamonds rare
Honor Chests Versatile exclusive equipment fragments From Alliance Duel, events
Presser Hunt Chests Speed ups, small diamonds Good for speed up stockpile
Hero Battlefield Honor Crate Up to 50 golden wrenches Wrenches are extremely rare
Dual Master Supply Box 10k diamonds, 3k badges (0.01%) Jackpot chest — very rare
Component Chests Level 1 component boxes (~8% drop) From various sources

Chest Opening Strategy

  • Save chests — avoid daily trickle opening if you are building a larger reserve
  • Open in bulk — same odds per chest, but more total chances at rare drops
  • Bounty chests: most results are resource boxes; diamond drops are the exception
  • Realistic expectations: 10k diamonds from 1,000+ chests is lucky, not normal

Real Example (from 10k+ chest opening)

  • ~20k diamonds total (including 10k and 5k jackpots)
  • ~40 universal orange hero fragments
  • 6k+ badges
  • 44 level 1 component boxes
  • Days worth of speed ups (construction, training, research)

Note: This required saving chests for weeks. Don't expect these results from daily opening.

How to Get Steel Fast

Plan steel separately from food, wood, and electricity. When HQ and late-game systems start asking for it, orange bounties, Steel Mine, steel nodes, shops, Furylord, and event rewards become the routine. See the dedicated Steel Guide for the full breakdown.

1. Orange Bounty Missions

  • Prioritize orange bounties once steel is active on your server
  • Use refreshes carefully when you need steel for HQ31-35
  • Confirm the reward preview before spending refresh items

2. Steel Mine and Steel Nodes

  • Gather from steel nodes once they appear at higher levels
  • Use Steel Mine output as steady background income

3. Shops, Furylord, Events, and Tasks

  • Alliance Duel rewards
  • Special events occasionally give Steel
  • Daily/weekly task rewards

Critical tip: check the live reward screen before counting any event as a steel source. Tyrant is still useful for alliance rewards and Power Cores, but do not plan steel around it unless the game shows steel.

How to Get Diamonds

Diamonds are reserve currency, not just another resource. Saved chests can create big spikes, but regular diamond income still comes from tasks, achievements, events, arena, and codes.

For the bigger question of what those diamonds should stay reserved for before you spend them, use the Diamond Reserve Guide.

Regular Diamond Sources

  • Daily Tasks — Small but consistent
  • Achievements — One-time rewards
  • Events — Alliance Duel, Full Preparedness, special events
  • Arena Rankings — Top 50 gives extra rewards
  • Redeem Codes — Check our Codes page for free diamonds

Diamond Jackpots from Chests

Chest Type Diamond Reward Probability
Bounty Mission Chest 100 - 5,000 ~10% for any, 5k very rare
Dual Master Supply Box 10,000 0.01% (jackpot)
Presser Hunt Chest 100 - 1,000 Uncommon

Tip: Save bounty mission chests for weeks, then open thousands at once. One player got 20k diamonds from bulk opening (including 10k + 5k jackpots).

How to Spend Diamonds Wisely

  • Do: keep a reserve for Lucky Discounter, Gacha Go, and other high-value event windows
  • Do: compare VIP shop and Alliance Shop items when the account needs shields, teleports, speedups, wrenches, badges, or other bottleneck materials
  • Do not: speed up random timers just because diamonds are available
  • Do not: buy random chests when the same chest type can be earned through routine play

How to Protect Resources from Raids

Use Warehouse protection as a safety layer, not as a reason to hoard. The practical rule is simple: spend exposed resources before logging off, keep your stash under the Warehouse protection limit when possible, and use a shield when you cannot reduce the risk.

Warehouse Upgrades

Your Warehouse protects a portion of resources from raids. Upgrade it when you are repeatedly carrying resources above the protected limit, when an HQ or event requirement forces it, or when your account needs more offline safety.

  • Check the protected limit before long offline windows
  • Spend resources above the limit on upgrades, research, or training before logging off
  • Do not over-prioritize Warehouse if gathering, HQ prerequisites, combat buildings, or shield planning are more urgent
  • Use shields when your exposed resources are high and you cannot defend or spend them cleanly

Protection Strategies

  • Spend before logging off — Start long upgrades/research
  • Use shields — When you have large amounts saved
  • Join NAP alliance — Non-Aggression Pact = less raids
  • Stay in alliance territory — Harder for enemies to reach
  • Don't hoard — Spend resources as you get them

For the weekly protection routine behind those choices, use the Shield Strategy guide.

When to Use Farm Accounts

Do not start a farm account just because you are new. Add one when your main account repeatedly runs out of food, wood, or electricity even after full marches and daily tasks. For setup, use the Farm Account Guide.

What is a Farm Account?

  • Secondary account focused mostly on gathering and resource support
  • Feeds extra resources toward your main account through alliance systems
  • Useful for F2P late-game shortages, but not mandatory for every player

How to Set Up

  1. Create new account on same server
  2. Join same alliance as main
  3. Focus on gathering buildings and march capacity
  4. Send gathered resources to main via alliance transfers

Tips for Farm Accounts

  • Keep HQ only as high as needed for gathering and transfer utility
  • Prioritize gathering research and march capacity
  • Start with one farm before adding more account upkeep
  • Avoid combat investment unless it directly protects the farm's resource role

Should You Build Resource Buildings?

Do not spend builder time on production buildings unless an HQ requirement forces it. Gathering creates the real resource flow; production buildings usually cost more attention than they return.

Why Resource Buildings Are Bad

  • Terrible output — produce very little per hour
  • Gathering is 10-50x better — one gathering trip = days of production
  • Waste builder time — builders should focus on HQ prerequisites
  • Waste resources — upgrading them costs more than they produce

What Buildings to Skip

  • Farmhouse (food production)
  • Residence (wood production)
  • Wind Turbine (electricity production)
  • Smelting Plant (steel production)

Exception: Only build these if required for HQ upgrade prerequisites. Keep them at minimum required level.

FAQ

How do I get more resources fast in Last Z?

Use full gathering marches, daily tasks, bounties, trucks, and events first. Save chests for bulk openings, and add farm accounts only after your main account has regular shortages.

Should I open chests daily or save them?

Usually save them. Bulk opening does not improve the odds per chest, but it gives more total chances at rare drops. Treat jackpot diamonds as a bonus, not guaranteed income.

Are resource buildings worth upgrading in Last Z?

Usually no, unless an HQ requirement forces them higher. Passive production is weak compared with gathering, so builder time is better spent on progression buildings.

What is the best way to get steel in Last Z?

For most players, practical steel sources are orange bounties, Steel Mine or steel nodes, Hub Shop, Furylord, seasonal rewards, and any active HQ31-35 launch event. Steel should be treated as a separate bottleneck from food, wood, and electricity.

What is a farm account in Last Z?

A farm account is a secondary account focused on gathering resources for your main account. It is most useful in later progression when normal income is no longer enough.

How do I get free diamonds?

Use daily tasks, achievements, events, arena rankings, redeem codes, and saved chest openings. Keep diamonds reserve-first before spending them on Lucky Discounter, Gacha Go, or other high-value event windows.
