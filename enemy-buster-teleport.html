Last Z Enemy Buster Teleport Guide

PvP support guide • Updated June 19, 2026 • Last Z Guides

Quick Answer

Enemy Buster teleport is the free Saturday server teleport that opens during Enemy Buster Day in Alliance Duel. It lets you teleport to the opposing Alliance Duel server and choose any map location there without spending Advanced Teleporters, Alliance Teleporters, or Random Teleporters.

  1. 1 Opens Saturday - only during Enemy Buster Day in Alliance Duel.
  2. 2 Costs nothing - use the free server teleport instead of normal teleport items.
  3. 3 Return rule - return through Alliance Duel and land randomly inside your alliance hive.
  4. 4 Wait 20 seconds - after returning before jumping back to the enemy server.

i Main uses: free positioning around SVS/Capital fights and scouting weak resource targets on the opponent server.

When Enemy Buster Teleport Opens

Enemy Buster teleport is available only on Saturday, during Enemy Buster Day in Alliance Duel. It is tied to the weekly Alliance Duel opponent, not to normal map movement.

This is why it should not be treated like a normal teleport item. During the active Saturday window, the game gives you a free way to move to the enemy Alliance Duel server and back.

How to Use the Free Teleport

There are two practical ways to use it:

  1. Open the Alliance Duel tab and use the Enemy Buster teleport option from there.
  2. Search or switch to the opponent server, pick a location on the map, and teleport there.

The important value is that the teleport is free. You do not spend Advanced Teleporters, Alliance Teleporters, or Random Teleporters while using this Enemy Buster/Alliance Duel server teleport.

How Returning Works

Returning home is the part players can mess up.

To return for free, use the Alliance Duel tab. The return teleport does not let you choose an exact tile. It places you randomly inside your alliance hive.

After you return, you must wait 20 seconds before teleporting back to the enemy server again. Plan around that cooldown if you are jumping between scouting, attacks, and regrouping.

Why It Is Different From Normal Teleports

Normal teleports are limited items. If you move around with Advanced Teleporters, Alliance Teleporters, or Random Teleporters, every mistake costs inventory.

Enemy Buster teleport is different because Saturday movement to the opponent server is free. That makes it useful for testing positions, moving around the enemy map, joining better attack angles, or leaving a bad spot without burning your normal teleport stock.

Best Use 1: SVS Capital Positioning

The strongest strategic use is during SVS-style Saturday fighting around Capital.

The attacking side can use free Enemy Buster teleports to take useful positions near Capital, relocate after pressure shifts, and occupy open spots in the enemy defense without spending normal teleports. This matters because map position changes quickly during Capital fights, and free repositioning lets active players react without draining teleport items.

Best Use 2: Resource Farming

The more common use is farming on the enemy server.

You can teleport over, scan the map, scout many HQs, and look for weak players holding a lot of resources. The goal is not random attacks. The goal is to find safe, efficient targets where the resource gain is worth the risk.

Scout first. Avoid strong alliances, obvious traps, and targets that can punish you. If the target looks bad, move on; the teleport movement is free, but troop losses are not.

Mistakes to Avoid

  • Do not spend normal teleport items when the free Enemy Buster teleport is available.
  • Do not forget that the free return places you randomly inside your alliance hive, not on a tile you choose.
  • Do not jump to the enemy server without checking shield timing, hospital capacity, troop risk, and whether you can stay active.
  • Do not farm blindly. Scout multiple HQs and only hit targets where the reward is worth the risk.
  • Do not assume this works outside Saturday Enemy Buster Day. This is an Alliance Duel Saturday mechanic.

Before You Jump Servers

  • Check the live Alliance Duel screen first; this teleport is tied to Saturday Enemy Buster Day.
  • Use the free return through Alliance Duel if you want the no-cost return path.
  • If you cannot stay active, use a shield plan instead of treating free movement as protection.
