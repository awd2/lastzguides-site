Last Z Military Strategies Costs — Total Badge Cost and Recharge Shield Path

Research Costs • Updated March 15, 2026 • Branch breakdown • Last Z Guides

Quick Answer

Best Military Strategies rule for most players: this is a branch you usually do not skip. The full path costs 79,816 badges, Recharge Shield arrives early at 22,096 cumulative badges, and the branch unlocks both Peace Shield and Siege to Seize.

  1. ① Full branch cost · 79,816 badges
  2. ② Key unlock · Recharge Shield at 22,096 cumulative badges
  3. ③ What it opens · Peace Shield at 45% and Siege to Seize at 40%

💡 Key note: Military Strategies is one of the cleanest examples of good badge efficiency: it is cheap, useful, and directly unlocks stronger research later.

Military Strategies Summary

Military Strategies is a compact combat branch that gives efficient troop-value upgrades before the expensive late game trees begin. It is one of the strongest badge investments for players who want useful progress instead of prestige completion.

  • Total cost: 79,816 badges
  • Branch size: 18 nodes / 149 total levels
  • Key unlock: Recharge Shield
  • Unlock requirement: 100% Elite Troops

Unlock Requirements and Why This Branch Matters

Military Strategies unlocks after you complete Elite Troops and then becomes one of the most important bridge branches in the whole combat tree.

  • Unlock requirement: 100% Elite Troops.
  • 40% Military Strategies unlocks Siege to Seize.
  • 45% Military Strategies plus 50% Shelter Building unlocks Peace Shield.

This is why Military Strategies usually comes right after Hero Training in the strongest research order for most accounts.

Key Military Strategies Checkpoints

Early opener — 4,258 badges

Fire Up and Armor Upgrade together cost only 4,258 badges, so the branch starts cheaply.

First full combat block — 14,176 badges

After the first six ten-level nodes, you already have the core cheap value that makes Military Strategies so efficient.

Recharge Shield reached — 22,096 badges

Recharge Shield arrives early and is one of the main reasons this branch pays back its badge cost so well.

Full branch — 79,816 badges

Even fully completed, Military Strategies stays far cheaper than Peace Shield, Army Building, or Unit Special Training.

What Comes Next After Military Strategies

For most players, Military Strategies is not a branch you rush past. It is usually worth doing properly because it gives efficient combat value and opens two critical next branches: Peace Shield and Siege to Seize.

If your next goal is survivability and loss reduction, move into Peace Shield Costs. If you are planning the more aggressive path or want the unlock route toward Field Research, move into Siege to Seize Costs.

Military Strategies FAQ

How many badges does Military Strategies cost in Last Z?

Military Strategies costs 79,816 badges in total. That is very cheap compared with deeper combat branches, which is one reason it is such a strong early investment.

Is Military Strategies worth finishing?

Usually yes. The branch is efficient, gives practical combat value, and unlocks both Peace Shield and Siege to Seize.

What does Military Strategies unlock?

Military Strategies unlocks Siege to Seize at 40% progress and Peace Shield at 45% progress when your Shelter Building branch is also at 50%.

Where is Recharge Shield in Military Strategies?

Recharge Shield sits in the middle of the branch and arrives at 22,096 cumulative badges. It is one of the strongest value checkpoints on the tree.

Is Military Strategies better than pushing Hero Training further?

Usually yes. Once Cockpit is done, Military Strategies gives stronger badge efficiency than spending more badges just to finish Hero Training.
