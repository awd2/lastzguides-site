Last Z Hero Recruitment Guide

Heroes • Last Z Hero Recruitment Guide • Updated May 2026 • Last Z Guides

Quick Answer

Best Last Z hero recruitment rule: unlock the Club at Stage 7, then treat Prime Recruitment Tickets and hero fragments as event-timed resources. Use them for your main 5 heroes first, and try to line big hero spending up with Day 4 (Thursday): Hero Initiative in Alliance Duel or Hero Initiative windows in Full Preparedness.

  1. ①Tickets·save Prime Recruitment Tickets when a useful hero scoring window is close.
  2. ②Fragments·use hero fragments on heroes that belong in your main formation first.
  3. ③Avoid·spending EXP, books, fragments, or gear on every new hero you pull.

This page explains recruitment timing and hero-material discipline. For hero rankings and faction choices, use the Heroes Tier List.

How to Recruit Without Wasting Tickets

Use this sequence when deciding whether to recruit now or wait:

  1. Confirm that the Club is open.
  2. Check the event schedule: Day 4 (Thursday): Hero Initiative in Alliance Duel is the main weekly hero-spending day, while Full Preparedness can add shorter Hero Initiative windows.
  3. If no useful timing is close, save Prime Recruitment Tickets unless your roster is blocking progress right now.
  4. When you recruit, compare new heroes against your actual main formation instead of upgrading everything you pull.
  5. Spend hero fragments on heroes you expect to keep using, especially your main 5 combat heroes.

The goal is to turn recruitment into formation progress and event value, not just to clear a red dot.

Common Hero Recruitment Mistakes

  • Recruiting the moment tickets arrive: this can miss points if Day 4 (Thursday): Hero Initiative or a Full Preparedness Hero Initiative window is close.
  • Upgrading every recruited hero: hero EXP, fragments, skill books, power cores, and gear should stay focused on the main formation.
  • Confusing tickets and fragments: tickets recruit heroes or hero pieces; fragments are upgrade material after you have the hero or can use versatile fragments.
  • Trusting old odds or ticket lists: this guide does not publish pull odds, drop rates, or a permanent list of every ticket source unless that information is clearly confirmed.

Recruitment Checklist

  1. Check unlock: if the Club is not open yet, keep pushing early stages.
  2. Check Alliance Duel: if Day 4 (Thursday): Hero Initiative is close, wait unless your roster is blocking progress right now.
  3. Check Full Preparedness: if a Hero Initiative window is active or coming soon, time upgrades there when practical.
  4. Check your formation: know which 5 heroes you are actually building before spending fragments.
  5. Check after recruiting: route back to the Heroes guide before spending major hero materials.

Related Next Steps
