Last Z Gift Center Login and UID Setup — Official Redeem Page

Support Guide • Updated March 2026 • Official Gift Center flow • Last Z Guides

Quick Answer

Official Last Z Gift Center setup: open the Gift Center in a browser, copy your UID from Avatar → Settings → Copy ID, paste the code outside the game, and collect rewards from your in-game mailbox.

  1. ① Official page · Gift Center login
  2. ② Copy UID · Avatar → Settings → Copy ID
  3. ③ Mobile flow · browser on iPhone or Android
  4. ④ Rewards · collect from in-game mailbox

🔐 Official only: codes are redeemed on the Gift Center website, not inside the game app.

🆔 Safest UID method: copy your ID directly from Settings instead of typing it manually.

📬 Mailbox rule: rewards appear in your in-game mail, not on the Gift Center screen.

How to Find and Copy Your UID

Your UID is required for every redemption. The cleanest method is to copy it directly in-game so you do not introduce typing mistakes.

  1. Open Last Z
  2. Tap your Avatar in the top-left corner
  3. Open Settings
  4. Tap Copy ID
  5. Paste that UID into the Gift Center

Can I Redeem Last Z Codes on iPhone or Android?

Yes. The redemption flow is the same on iPhone and Android because both use the official Gift Center website in a browser.

  • iPhone: open the Gift Center in Safari or another browser, then paste your UID and code.
  • Android: open the Gift Center in Chrome or another browser, then paste your UID and code.
  • In-game: not supported for code redemption.

Can You Redeem Codes Inside the Game?

No. Last Z codes are redeemed through the Gift Center website, not inside the game client. If you are trying to find an in-app redeem menu, you are looking in the wrong place.

Where Do Rewards Go After Redemption?

Rewards are sent to your in-game mailbox. After redeeming, open the game and check your mail. If rewards do not appear instantly, wait a few minutes and restart the game once.

Common Gift Center Setup Mistakes

  • Using the game client instead of the website
  • Typing the UID manually instead of copying it
  • Expecting rewards on the Gift Center page instead of checking mailbox
  • Forgetting that iPhone and Android both use a browser flow
