Last Z Peace Shield Costs — Urgent Rescue and Total Badge Cost

Research Costs • Updated March 15, 2026 • Branch breakdown • Last Z Guides

Quick Answer

Best Peace Shield rule for most players: this branch matters because of Urgent Rescue and the deep defensive HP path behind it. The full branch costs 267,613 badges, but its first truly game-changing checkpoint appears much earlier.

  1. ① Full branch cost · 267,613 badges
  2. ② Key unlock · Urgent Rescue at 39,913 cumulative badges
  3. ③ Best use · PvP survivability and lower unit-loss pressure

💡 Key note: If your server has real PvP pressure, Peace Shield is usually more important than badge spending on weaker prestige branches.

Peace Shield Summary

Peace Shield is one of the defining PvP branches in Last Z. It is much more expensive than Military Strategies, but its value is concentrated in unlocks that change real combat outcomes instead of only improving small paper stats.

  • Total cost: 267,613 badges
  • Branch size: 16 nodes / 165 total levels
  • Key unlock: Urgent Rescue
  • Final node: Recharge Shield

Unlock Requirements and Why This Branch Matters

Peace Shield is not an entry-level branch. It opens only after you have already built a strong foundation in Shelter Building and Military Strategies.

  • Unlock requirement: 50% Shelter Building.
  • Unlock requirement: 45% Military Strategies.
  • Army Building later requires 100% Peace Shield.

This is why Peace Shield usually comes after Military Strategies in the strongest research order, but before more aggressive branches when survivability matters.

Key Peace Shield Checkpoints

Early setup finished — 30,963 badges

By the time you clear the first nine nodes, you have already paid most of the price needed to reach Urgent Rescue.

Urgent Rescue reached — 39,913 badges

Urgent Rescue is the first major branch-defining unlock and the main reason many players push Peace Shield early.

Late defensive block — 73,723 badges

After the Stronger Armor, Shield Upgrade, and Tactical Cover trio, the branch starts transitioning into much more expensive late-game nodes.

Full branch — 267,613 badges

The two 20-level policy branches and final Recharge Shield are what turn Peace Shield into a serious long-term badge commitment.

What Comes Next After Peace Shield

Peace Shield is often the branch that separates "good paper stats" from research that changes real PvP outcomes. Once Urgent Rescue is secured, you can decide whether to finish the full branch or move toward Siege to Seize Costs depending on your server and playstyle.

If your goal is the later Army Building path, Peace Shield is also a prerequisite. Use the Army Building Costs page when you are planning beyond this branch.

Peace Shield FAQ

How many badges does Peace Shield cost in Last Z?

Peace Shield costs 267,613 badges in total. It is a much bigger commitment than Military Strategies, but it contains some of the strongest defensive value in the game.

Where is Urgent Rescue in Peace Shield?

Urgent Rescue sits in the middle of the branch and arrives at 39,913 cumulative badges. It is the first major reason many players push Peace Shield.

Is Peace Shield worth doing before Siege to Seize?

Usually yes. For most accounts, Peace Shield gives stronger real-world survivability and loss reduction than rushing into the more aggressive Siege to Seize path first.

What unlocks Peace Shield in Last Z?

Peace Shield requires 50% Shelter Building and 45% Military Strategies before it appears in your lab.

Does Peace Shield unlock Army Building?

Yes. Army Building requires 100% Peace Shield, which is one reason many late-game accounts eventually finish this branch.
