Best Fully Armed Alliance rule for most players: treat this as a late aggressive branch, not an early badge priority. The full path costs 492,920 badges, and most of its identity is tied to Annihilation and Move Out rather than efficient early value.

Fully Armed Alliance Summary

Fully Armed Alliance is a late aggressive combat branch with several distinct cost walls. The branch feels manageable at first, then quickly turns into a major badge commitment once Annihilation and Move Out enter the picture.

  • Total cost: 492,920 badges
  • Branch size: 18 nodes / 210 total levels
  • Key unlock: Annihilation
  • Final node: Move Out

Why This Branch Is Usually a Late Priority

Fully Armed Alliance is one of the easiest branches to romanticize and one of the easiest to overbuy too early.

  • The branch stays moderate early, then spikes hard at Annihilation and Move Out.
  • Most low-spender and F2P accounts should not treat it as an early badge priority.
  • It makes the most sense when your account is already built for aggressive combat play.

This is why many players leave Fully Armed Alliance for later even if the branch itself looks exciting.

Key Fully Armed Alliance Checkpoints

Early branch frame — 84,360 badges

By Peace Breaker, the branch still looks manageable, which is one reason players underestimate what comes next.

Annihilation reached — 148,820 badges

Annihilation is the first major branch-defining unlock and a big reason players talk about Fully Armed Alliance at all.

Post-Annihilation combat block — 367,720 badges

After the six troop training nodes and the mechanical-repair pair, the branch is already deep into late-game badge territory.

Full branch — 492,920 badges

Move Out alone adds 125,200 badges at the end, which makes the branch far more expensive than its early rows suggest.

How to Think About Fully Armed Alliance

Fully Armed Alliance is a branch for players who are already comfortable committing badges to aggressive late-game combat. If your account still needs stronger foundation branches, this is often not the best next research target.

Use the Tech Deep Dive and PvP Guide if you are deciding whether the account context really justifies an Annihilation or Move Out push.

Fully Armed Alliance FAQ

How many badges does Fully Armed Alliance cost in Last Z?

Fully Armed Alliance costs 492,920 badges in total. It is a large late-game branch and gets especially expensive at Annihilation and Move Out.

Where is Annihilation in Fully Armed Alliance?

Annihilation arrives at 148,820 cumulative badges and is one of the main reasons players care about this branch.

What is the final node in Fully Armed Alliance?

The final node is Move Out, which costs 125,200 badges by itself and brings the full branch total to 492,920 badges.

Is Fully Armed Alliance an early priority?

Usually no. Most accounts should handle cheaper and more efficient badge branches before Fully Armed Alliance becomes a sensible investment.

Why do players delay Fully Armed Alliance?

Because the early branch cost understates how expensive the late branch really becomes. Annihilation and especially Move Out turn it into a serious badge sink.
