Last Z Farm Account Guide — Same Server Setup Before Season 2

Economy Guide • Updated February 1, 2026 • 6 min read • Last Z Guides

Quick Answer

How to create a Last Z farm account: make a new account with a separate email, unlock the Profile menu, use Character Management to select your main server, and do it before Season 2 starts if your server still allows new farm creation.

  1. ① Create a new account · separate email + device/emulator
  2. ② Unlock Profile menu · play 5-10 minutes
  3. ③ Account → Character Management · create new character
  4. ④ Select your server · before Season 2
  5. ⑤ Join an alliance · farm alliance or main alliance

ℹ️ Key timing rule: community reports say new farm accounts on the same server may be blocked once Season 2 starts, so earlier setup is safer.

🌾 Main purpose: a farm account feeds resources to your main HQ and helps smooth progression.

🧭 Server rule: the farm must be created on your main server, not left on the starter server.

What Is a Farm Account in Last Z?

A farm account is a secondary character created to gather resources and support your main account. The goal is not to build a second competitive account, but to create a steady resource source that makes your main HQ progression easier.

How to Create a Farm Account on Your Server

If you want a farm alt on the same server as your main account, the important step is Character Management. Creating the new account is easy; placing that farm on the correct server before seasonal restrictions matter is the part most players care about.

Create a new account with a separate email, play until the Profile menu unlocks, then go to Profile → Account → Character Management and select your main server. Finish the tutorial and join an alliance. The full walkthrough is below in Step-by-Step Setup.

Why You Should Create a Farm Before Season 2

The main reason players search for farm account timing is Season 2. Community reports suggest new farm creation on the same server may become restricted after Season 2 begins, so waiting too long can remove the option entirely.

For newer servers, Season 2 is Winter. If you see older guides calling Season 2 Desert, use the Season 2 Winter clarification before planning around old season names.

What You Need to Set Up a Farm Account

  • A separate email for the new account
  • A second device or emulator (phone, tablet, or PC). Popular options include LDPlayer or BlueStacks.
  • 5-10 minutes to unlock the Profile menu

Farm Account Setup Step by Step

If this is still a new account environment for you, the easiest way to keep both accounts organized is to follow a simple routine for tasks, gathering, and alliance activity. The Daily Checklist and Beginner's Guide help with that side of the setup.

  1. Install Last Z on a second device or emulator and sign in with a new email.
  2. Play for a few minutes until the Profile icon appears in the top-left.
  3. Open Profile → Account → Character Management.
  4. Select Create New Character, then choose your main server.
  5. Finish the tutorial and join a farm alliance or your main alliance.

What to Do After You Create the Farm

Once the farm is created, the goal is consistency. Keep it active, collect resources, join an alliance for passive gains and protection, and treat it as support infrastructure for your main account rather than a second progression project.

  • Keep it active with basic tasks and resource collection.
  • Join an alliance to gain gifts and protection.
  • Support your main HQ by providing resources when needed. Use the HQ Upgrade Guide, Resources Guide, and F2P Guide to decide where the farm helps most.
  • Optional: delete the starter character created on another server if you will never use it.

FAQ

What is a farm account used for in Last Z?

A farm account is used to gather resources and support your main HQ. Its job is to improve your main account's progression, not to become a second fully developed main.

Can I create a farm account after Season 2 starts?

Community reports say new farms must be created before Season 2 begins on your server. After Season 2 starts, creating a new character in that state may be blocked.

Do I need a separate email for a farm account?

Yes — using a separate email is the standard way to create another account on the same server.

How do I create a farm account on my server?

Create a new account with a separate email, unlock the Profile menu, open Character Management, and select your main server before finishing setup.

Does a farm account need to be on the same server as my main account?

Yes, if you want it to support your main account directly. A farm left on another server will not help your main server progression.

What should I do after creating the farm account?

Join a farm alliance or your main alliance, keep the account active for resource gains, and use it to support your main Headquarters (HQ).

Before Building a Farm Account

  • Checked with server 403 players: farms are useful when they support your main account, not when they become a second main.
  • Create the farm on the same server as your main before Season 2 if your server still allows it.
  • Before investing time, check the current account and character rules in game.
