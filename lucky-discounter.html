What Is Lucky Discounter in Last Z?

Lucky Discounter is a diamond-spend event where saved tickets let you refresh discounted shop offers. The event is strongest when you plan around fixed 90% checkpoints instead of buying every random discount.

Main target: badges. They feed Alliance Recognition research, medal production, hero training speed, and other long-term account bonuses.

If you are buying badges for long-term progression, use the Alliance Recognition Costs guide to see how far those badge purchases actually move your account.

Lucky Discounter Schedule and Rotation

If you are searching for when Lucky Discounter appears, the important point is that it follows a repeating event rotation. That makes it easier to plan both tickets and diamonds in advance instead of reacting when the event suddenly appears.

Lucky Discounter follows a consistent weekly rotation: Gacha Go → Bullseye → Lucky Discounter → Lucky Chest. Plan tickets and diamonds around this cycle so you can hit the guaranteed 90% attempts when Lucky Discounter returns.

For the wider event calendar and rotation planning, use the Events Schedule. If you want the bigger reserve logic behind shield diamonds versus event diamonds, use the Diamond Reserve Guide.

Guaranteed 90% Discount Attempts

The most important part of Lucky Discounter is that several attempts are fixed, not random. Those guaranteed 90% discounts are what turn the event from maybe useful into one of the best badge-buying opportunities in the game.

How Many Lucky Discounter Tickets Should You Save?

Most players do better by saving tickets for bulk value instead of spending them immediately. The event becomes far more predictable once you plan around known 90% checkpoints.

Minimum Viable (9 tickets)

  • Guarantees two 90% discounts (5th and 9th)
  • Good for F2P with limited patience
  • Accumulates in ~2 months

Recommended (18 tickets)

  • Guarantees three 90% discounts (5th, 9th, 18th)
  • Best balance of waiting vs rewards
  • Accumulates in ~4-5 months

Maximum Value (32 tickets)

  • Guarantees four 90% discounts (5th, 9th, 18th, 32nd)
  • Requires ~8 months of saving
  • Best for patient endgame players

Tip: You get roughly 4-5 tickets per event cycle. Save them until you hit your target number.

Why Badges Are the Best Lucky Discounter Purchase

Badges are usually the best Lucky Discounter purchase because they feed high-value long-term research instead of short-term convenience. For most accounts, that makes them the strongest event target.

Badges fuel Alliance Recognition research, which provides:

  • Medal production (for hero upgrades)
  • Hero training speed bonuses
  • Alliance buffs and bonuses
  • Critical late-game power multipliers

You'll need 50,000-100,000+ badges to complete Alliance Recognition research. Lucky Discounter is the fastest F2P path to accumulating them. Use badges strategically during Alliance Duel Day 3 for maximum value.

FAQ

What is the best Lucky Discounter strategy in Last Z?

Save at least 9 tickets, wait for guaranteed 90% attempts, prioritize badges first, and make sure you have enough diamonds before the event starts.

When is Lucky Discounter in Last Z?

It appears in the weekly rotation: Gacha Go → Bullseye → Lucky Discounter → Lucky Chest. Plan tickets and diamonds around that cycle.

What are the guaranteed 90% Lucky Discounter attempts?

The guaranteed 90% discounts happen on attempts 5, 9, 18, and 32. These are fixed checkpoints and do not depend on luck.

Do refresh tickets expire?

No, tickets carry over between events. You can save them for months.

Is Lucky Discounter worth it in Last Z: Survival Shooter?

Yes. Lucky Discounter is one of the best F2P badge events in the game, especially if you save tickets and only spend heavily around guaranteed 90% attempts.

What if I don't have enough diamonds when 90% appears?

This is why preparation matters. Collect diamonds before the event starts. Missing a 90% discount is a significant loss.

Is Lucky Discounter 50% discount worth it for badges?

Yes if you have spare diamonds, but 90% is the priority. Treat 50% as a fallback.

How many tickets should I save for Lucky Discounter?

Save at least 9 tickets for two guaranteed 90% attempts. 18 tickets is a stronger long-term target, and 32 tickets is the maximum-value plan for very patient players.

Should I spend diamonds before I hit 90%?

Usually no. Most of the value comes from guaranteed 90% attempts, so spending too early often lowers total badge efficiency.

What is the best item to buy in Lucky Discounter?

For most players, badges are the best purchase because they feed long-term research and progression instead of short-term convenience value.
