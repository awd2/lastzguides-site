Yu Chan Guide — Skills & Best Build

Heroes Guide • Updated February 22, 2026 • 7 min read • Last Z Guides • Checked with server 403 players

Quick Answer

Yu Chan is a Blood Rose DPS hero. Her damage scales through Death’s Aim (normal attacks), Soul Striker (burst), and Wyrm Wrath (self skill damage). Her exclusive talent Dragon’s Bane gives Blood Rose troops +10% damage vs Riders and 10% less damage from Shooters.

💡 Best use: Blood Rose core with Assaulter troops.

Step 1: Role & Faction

Yu Chan is a Blood Rose DPS hero designed for sustained damage and burst. She performs best in a Blood Rose core with Assaulter troop alignment.

Step 2: Skill Order & Max Effects

1) Death’s Aim — Normal Attack Boost (Max)

Max effect: Normal attacks’ damage factor increases to 1461.

  • ★ +120
  • ★★ +120
  • ★★★ +120
  • ★★★★ +150
  • ★★★★★ +300

2) Soul Striker — Active Skill (Max)

Max effect: Damage Factor 1655.

  • ★ Fire Count +1
  • ★★ Damage Factor +200
  • ★★★ Damage Factor +200
  • ★★★★ Fire Count +2
  • ★★★★★ Damage Factor +400

3) Wyrm Wrath — Skill DMG (Self) (Max)

Max effect: Skill DMG (Self) +16.9%.

  • ★ +1.5%
  • ★★ +1.5%
  • ★★★ +2%
  • ★★★★ +2%
  • ★★★★★ +3%

4) Dragon’s Bane — Exclusive Talent (Max)

Effect: [Wyrm Wrath] applies to all in‑troop Blood Rose heroes. Troops deal +10% damage to enemy Rider units and take 10% less damage from Shooter units.

Step 3: Best Gear & Stat Priority

Prioritize Attack % → Damage % → HP % for DPS scaling. Focus gear on your main 5 heroes only. See the Gear Guide for upgrade priority.

Step 4: Team Synergy

Yu Chan performs best with a Blood Rose core. Keep troop alignment to Assaulters and build around faction synergy. See Best Formations for 3+2 options.

Step 5: Investment Rules

Do not spread resources across non-core heroes. Keep Yu Chan in your main DPS slot and upgrade skills, gear, and stars evenly with the rest of your core team.

Tips & Common Mistakes

  • Stay in Blood Rose — faction synergy is a major power multiplier
  • Assaulter alignment — match troops to faction
  • Don’t split gear — focus on your main 5 heroes
  • Prioritize Death’s Aim — best sustained DPS scaling

FAQ

What is Yu Chan’s exclusive talent?

Dragon’s Bane applies to all in‑troop Blood Rose heroes, giving +10% damage to enemy Rider units and 10% less damage from Shooter units.

What is the best skill priority for Yu Chan?

Prioritize Death’s Aim for sustained damage, then Soul Striker for burst, followed by Wyrm Wrath.

Is Yu Chan a must-have for Blood Rose?

If you run a Blood Rose core, Yu Chan is a main DPS and scales strongly with faction synergy.
