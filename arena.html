Last Z Arena Guide — Daily Rank, Weekly Rank, Valor Medals, and Merit Shop

PvP Guide • Updated March 15, 2026 • 5 min read • Last Z Guides • Checked with server 403 players

Quick Answer

Best Last Z arena strategy: use all 5 free attempts every day, refresh for easier wins, push daily and weekly rank as high as you can, then spend Valor Medals in Merit Shop on Orange Equipment, discounted Power Cores, and discounted Forging Stones.

How Arena Works in Last Z

Arena is a recurring value system, not just a vanity ladder. Its real strength comes from repeated daily participation, better daily and weekly rank rewards, and Merit Shop purchases with Valor Medals that feed account growth.

That means the best arena mindset is simple: use your free attempts, avoid bad fights, and protect consistency instead of chasing random hero-power screenshots.

Best Daily Arena Routine

The strongest daily arena routine for most players is not complicated. Use all 5 free attempts every day, refresh for easier targets, and take wins that preserve your weekly value.

  • Use all 5 free attempts — free attempts should never sit unused
  • Use free refresh — easy wins count the same as difficult ones
  • Avoid ego fights — losing to stronger opponents lowers your weekly efficiency

For the wider daily routine around arena, radar, trucks, and other recurring tasks, use the Daily Checklist.

How to Pick Better Arena Targets

Target selection matters more than forcing perfect formations for most accounts. Arena is often won by picking the right opponents, not just by brute power.

Use free refresh to find weaker or less optimized targets. If a fight looks close and risky, it is usually better to refresh again than to waste an attempt on a low-probability win.

For better hero and formation choices behind those fights, use the Heroes Guide.

Why Arena Matters for Weekly Value

Arena is one of the cleaner recurring sources of weekly value because it rewards discipline instead of one-time spending spikes. That is especially useful for F2P and low-spender accounts that need steady Valor Medals and rank rewards.

It also feeds your broader progression loop, because daily and weekly rank rewards turn into Merit Shop value over time. For the bigger power picture, use the Power Guide.

What to Buy in the Merit Shop with Valor Medals

Arena rank rewards give Valor Medals, and those medals should be treated as long-term account growth currency. The best Merit Shop buys are the items that keep improving your roster instead of disappearing into short-term filler.

For most accounts, the best Valor Medal priority is:

  • First: Orange Equipment
  • Next: discounted Power Cores
  • Next: discounted Forging Stones
  • Skip: filler buys that do not improve gear, core power, or weekly Arena value

Common Arena Mistakes

  • Skipping free attempts — this is the easiest way to lose recurring value
  • Fighting targets that are too strong — ego fights are usually worse than steady wins
  • Ignoring weekly rank value — arena should be judged across the week, not one match at a time
  • Spending diamonds on extra attempts too casually — for most players, free attempts are enough
  • Treating Valor Medals casually — Orange Equipment, discounted Power Cores, and discounted Forging Stones matter more than random spending

Arena and F2P Progression Value

Arena matters for F2P not because it is flashy, but because daily and weekly rank rewards stack with other recurring systems like events, Furylord, and redeem codes. Small and medium income sources add up when you stay consistent.

For the bigger free-diamond picture and how arena fits into it, use the F2P Guide.

Frequently Asked Questions

How many arena attempts should I use in Last Z every day?

Use all 5 free arena attempts every day. Arena is one of the easiest recurring sources of steady weekly value.

Should I refresh for easier arena targets?

Yes. Free refreshes are usually worth using because easy wins count the same as risky fights and protect your weekly arena value.

Why does arena matter for F2P players?

Arena matters because repeated daily participation improves daily and weekly rank rewards and helps you earn more Valor Medals without requiring a heavy one-time spend.

What should I buy first with Valor Medals?

Use Valor Medals in Merit Shop on Orange Equipment first, then discounted Power Cores and discounted Forging Stones. Those buys give better long-term account value than random filler items.

Should I spend diamonds on extra arena attempts?

Usually no for most players. The 5 free daily attempts already give strong recurring value, and diamonds are usually better saved for higher-impact event spending.
