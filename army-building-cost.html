Last Z Army Building Costs — Total Badge Cost and Peace Shield Follow-Up

Research Costs • Updated March 15, 2026 • Branch breakdown • Last Z Guides

Quick Answer

Best Army Building rule for most players: do not treat this as an early branch. The full path costs 627,130 badges, requires 100% Peace Shield, and only starts to make sense once your earlier research value is already secured.

  1. ① Full branch cost · 627,130 badges
  2. ② Unlock requirement · 100% Peace Shield
  3. ③ Best use · Late-game combat scaling, not early efficiency

💡 Key note: Army Building is where badge planning stops being about cheap efficiency and starts becoming a real endgame account decision.

Army Building Summary

Army Building is a large follow-up branch that only opens once Peace Shield is fully complete. It adds a lot of combat weight, but it is far enough into the tree that many accounts should think carefully before moving into it.

  • Total cost: 627,130 badges
  • Branch size: 23 nodes / 256 total levels
  • Unlock requirement: 100% Peace Shield
  • Final node: Recharge Shield

Unlock Requirements and Why This Branch Matters

Army Building only appears once Peace Shield is fully complete, which already makes it a late-game branch by definition.

  • Unlock requirement: 100% Peace Shield.
  • This branch is a true follow-up, not an alternative early path.
  • The biggest cost walls arrive well after the opening nodes.

That is why Army Building is usually planned as part of a late combat roadmap rather than a broad early recommendation.

Key Army Building Checkpoints

Opening structure cleared — 29,230 badges

The first eight nodes are still relatively controlled and mostly serve as setup before the real branch cost begins.

Mid combat block finished — 172,030 badges

After the two three-node combat clusters, Army Building has already become a major badge commitment.

Second major wall — 463,690 badges

The repeated 15-level branches are where Army Building starts to feel like a true late-game cost wall.

Full branch — 627,130 badges

The late Fire Up, Armor Upgrade, and final Recharge Shield turn Army Building into one of the site's larger research commitments.

How to Use Army Building in Your Research Plan

Army Building is less about early efficiency and more about whether your account is already ready for a serious late-game badge branch. It only makes sense after Peace Shield is fully done and your earlier value branches are already locked in.

If you are still comparing whether to push Army Building or something even deeper, use the Unit Special Training Costs page to compare the next major badge wall.

Army Building FAQ

How many badges does Army Building cost in Last Z?

Army Building costs 627,130 badges in total. It is a large late-game branch and should be planned as a major badge commitment.

What unlocks Army Building?

Army Building requires 100% Peace Shield before it becomes available.

Is Army Building an early priority?

No. Army Building is a late Peace Shield follow-up branch and usually only makes sense once earlier high-efficiency branches are already done.

What is the final node in Army Building?

The final node is Recharge Shield, which costs 24,400 badges on top of a branch that is already very expensive before it.

Is Army Building cheaper than Unit Special Training?

Yes. Army Building is still expensive, but it is far cheaper than Unit Special Training and sits earlier in the late-game research ladder.

Before Pushing Army Building

  • Use the 627,130 badge total to compare this branch with Unit Special Training before committing to the late-game path.
